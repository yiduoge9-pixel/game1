Game introduction

Intro Game
Interactive experience

Choose the word most likely to be censored

This mini game invites players to reflect on how women’s health language is often treated differently on digital platforms.

How it works

You will see four words in each round. Choose the word most likely to be flagged, suppressed, or treated as “sensitive”. Then submit your answer to reveal the result.

What is this game about?

You will see four words in each round. Your task is to choose the one that is most likely to be flagged, suppressed, or treated as “sensitive” by moderation systems.

How to play

  • Read the four options carefully.
  • Select the word you think is most likely to be censored.
  • Click Submit to see whether your choice is correct.
  • Continue until you finish all question sets.

Why does it matter?

Many words related to women’s health, anatomy, reproduction, and care are unfairly moderated online. This shapes what people can say, learn, and share.

What you will discover

By playing, you will notice that perfectly normal educational or medical terms can be treated as risky content. The game turns that invisible filtering into something visible.

This experience is designed as a quick awareness activity. It does not represent a real platform moderation model, but helps illustrate common patterns of suppression.
